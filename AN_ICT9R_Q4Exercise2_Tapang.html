WHAT IS THE DIFFERENCE?

💥JAVA is composed of the following elements: server-side development, compiled language, static typed language, class-based, and multi-threaded.

💥 On the other hand, JAVASCRIPT is a widely used scripting language on the internet. Recognized and works in all major browsers designed to add interactivity and functionality to the website.

💥 Furthermore, it has the opposite characteristics of Java, consisting of the following elements: client-side scripts, interpreted scripted language, dynamic typed language, prototype-based, and single-threaded.
